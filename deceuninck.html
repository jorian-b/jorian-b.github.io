Deceuninck
VR training

The extrustion division startup module

My contribution to the VR training module for Deceuninck's extrusion division extended to the development of versatile systems designed to optimize the training experience. Through the implementation of these systems, I enabled the creation of multiple training scenarios within a single scene, enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of the training program.

The training content was structured into three main parts, each focusing on a distinct aspect of the extrusion process. Additionally, I integrated fifteen troubleshooting scenarios to guide trainees on how to address common issues that may arise during operations. These troubleshooting segments provide invaluable practical knowledge, equipping employees with the skills needed to navigate challenges effectively and maintain smooth workflow continuity.

By developing these systems, I aimed to provide comprehensive training that covers not only the standard procedures but also prepares employees to handle unexpected situations confidently. This approach ensures that trainees are well-equipped to contribute to operational success and uphold the high standards of quality and efficiency synonymous with Deceuninck.
