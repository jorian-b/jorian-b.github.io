Deceuninck
VR training

The extrustion division startup module

My contribution to the VR training module for Deceuninck's extrusion division was mainly the development of versatile systems to optimize the training experience. Through the implementation of these systems, we were able to create multiple training scenarios within a single scene, purely using data to change the training, enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of the training program.

The training content was structured into three main parts, each focusing on a distinct aspect of the extrusion process. After completing these, you could explore fifteen troubleshoot scenarios to guide trainees on how to address common issues that may arise during operations. These troubleshooting segments provide invaluable practical knowledge, equipping employees with the skills needed to navigate challenges effectively and maintain smooth workflow continuity.
