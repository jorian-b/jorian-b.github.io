Modulab

Modulab offers users the freedom to configure their dream bathroom down to the finest detail. As part of the project, I developed a Unity application that seamlessly interfaces with the website via API calls, generating the visual of the configured room in real time. This dynamic integration allows users to visualize their custom designs with ease, enhancing the overall experience of designing their ideal space.

Deceuninck VR Training

I contributed to the development of a VR training module for Deceuninck's extrusion division. This VR tool streamlines technical training, allowing employees to learn the extrusion process with standardized procedures, reduced training time, and minimized scrap production. The module offers various learning tools, including audio and visual elements, and localization.

AI Einstein Metahuman

In the Einstein Metahuman AI project, I created a communication system facilitating natural and engaging conversations with an AI version of Albert Einstein. The system utilizes advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) for seamless interaction, supports both text and voice communication. By creating a generated version of Einstein's voice, visual and personality traits, the project aims to offer users a distinctive and immersive experience, exploring the potential of human-like interactions with artificial intelligence.

Interactive Digital Graffiti Wall

The Interactive Digital Graffiti Wall project introduces a fusion of traditional graffiti art and modern technology. By attaching trackers to tools, users can paint directly on a digital screen, creating an interactive and engaging experience. The project aims to provide an innovative way for individuals to express their creativity, blending the physical act of painting with digital interactivity.
