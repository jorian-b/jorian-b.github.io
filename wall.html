Interactive Digital
Graffiti Wall

A project in cooperation with Tool of NA and nocomputer

The Digital Graffiti Wall is an interactive LED installation that merges creative expression with advanced technology. Developed in cooperation with Tool of NA and NoComputer, this project offers participants a unique platform to engage with digital art in real-time.

The installation provides participants with digital painting tools to interact with a blank canvas. Through the integration of hardware and software, users can apply color and shapes to the canvas, creating dynamic and collaborative artworks.



Powered by the HTC Vive Pro 2 headset, base stations, and Vive 3.0 trackers, the Digital Graffiti Wall offers a seamless and immersive experience. Custom hardware attachments, including paintbrushes and markers, enhance the realism of the interaction.

Participants engage with the Digital Graffiti Wall by manipulating the digital painting tools to apply strokes and shapes to the canvas. The software dynamically adjusts the behavior of the tools based on user input, ensuring a responsive and intuitive experience.
